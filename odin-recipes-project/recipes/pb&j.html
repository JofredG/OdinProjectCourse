How to make a Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description: It's a sandwich

  • Two slices of bread (preferably not the heel of course)
  • Jelly
  • Peanut Butter
  1. Toast the bread (optional)
  2. Using butter knife spread jelly on one of the slices
  3. Using butter knive spread peanut butter on the other slice
  4. Consolidate the two slices so that the spreads touch each other
  5. voila
