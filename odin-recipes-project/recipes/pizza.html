How to make a Pizza

Description: Savory crust with gooey cheese and delicious pepperoni slices.

Ingredients:

  • Plenty Dough
  • Plenty Mozzarella Cheese
  • Plenty Pepperoni

Steps:

  1. Heat the oven
  2. Form the pizza dough and place it on a peel dusted wiht a little flour or cornmeal.
  3. Slip the pizza onto the hot pizza stone
  4. After 5 minutes of baking, check the pizza
  5. The pizza is done when the cheese is melted to a medium-to-dark brown
